## üéâ Using resume-alchemist
When you open the application, you'll see a user-friendly interface. Follow these steps to start optimizing your resume:

1. **Upload Your Resume**: Click the "Upload" button and select your current resume file (PDF, Word, or TXT).
   
2. **Start Analysis**: After uploading, hit the "Analyze" button. The AI will work on your document and provide suggestions.

3. **Review Feedback**: Check the suggestions given by the application. Make changes directly in the resume or follow the advice for a complete overhaul.

4. **Use the STAR Method**: If your application prompts you for work experiences, refer to the STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) method for guidance. This method helps in structuring your experiences clearly.

5. **Download Your Updated Resume**: Once satisfied with the changes, click the "Download" button to save the optimized resume.
